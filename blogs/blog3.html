What are the different roles HTML, CSS, and javaScripts play?

This may come off a little disjointed and abstract but this is how I've been thinking about it!

They work together like a house
Html This is the frame of the foundation, frame, and location of the house.
CSS This works as the style and decoration of the house.
JavaSricpt This works as the functionality of the house.
The html is what we build off of. It's the base of what we are going to be seening, it doesnt have much style or functionality but we cant build of of nothing. JavaSricpt is all the behind the seens functionality, its the electrical work that makes the lights turn on and off when we need them too. It's the Ac unit that always keeps us comfortable, down to the hinges on the doors, we dont see it working in the background but its always running to keep the house working properly! Then we get to the CSS, This is the sytle of the house, this is the style of the windows and doors and where they all go! this also controloes the paint and the layout on the inside.
